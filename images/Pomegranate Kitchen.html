POMEGRANATE KITCHEN is a Hong Kong based Events and Catering company with two unique venues in Aberdeen.

We provide a full service from concept through to design and coordination, making your vision a reality.

We combine our passion for soulful and relaxed food with the flavours and aromas of the Mediterranean, fused with the scents and spices of South Asia. By using the best produce and experimental combinations of fingredients our team create fresh and original dishes.

Whether your event is at your office, home or one of our venues in Aberdeen, a cocktail reception or an 8 course fine dining experience, the Pomegranate team will create the perfect event for you.
